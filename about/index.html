About Us

About Plant Powered Chautauqua

We are part of the PlantPure Communities POD Network, which is a grassroots movement that helps build stronger, healthier communities. We welcome anyone who is interested in learning about plant-based nutrition and those currently living a WFPB lifestyle.

Plant Powered Chautauqua's Mission

We empower people to find better health and overcome chronic disease through a WFPB lifestyle. We offer evidence-based education, resources, and support to create community and inspire change! We strive to raise awareness about the dramatic health benefits of WFPB nutrition and to build supportive communities that empower people to make lifestyle changes.
