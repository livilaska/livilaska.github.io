what and why

How do I Start?

The food we eat has everything to do with how we feel! It also plays a huge role in developing or avoiding chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes-2, high blood pressure, and high cholesterol. Remember, WFPB is a lifestyle, not a diet. Starting a new way of eating to improve your life and health is a journey. Whether you want to start that journey by jumping in with both feet or ease your way in, we will be with you every step of the way!

If you are on medication and are changing to a whole food, plant-based diet, you should discuss with your healthcare provider the changes that you are making in your diet and how these changes may require an adjustment in medication dosage.
